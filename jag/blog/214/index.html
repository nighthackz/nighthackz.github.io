Trebuchet's and beer

I just about fell out of my chair when I saw this one:

Thanks to Andy Gelme for pointing it out:

... every time I see it, I'm reminded of your fascination for trebuchets.

Not many advertisements involve using a trebuchet to launch all of the important beer making ingredients (including naked virgins !) into the sky, in the hope that it'll rain beer !

It was filmed in the city of Melbourne, just a few blocks away from where I'm currently working.

As far as I know, the virgins never returned :)
