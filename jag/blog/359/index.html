Hudson is dead, long live Jenkins

I was relieved to read that the bizarre saga of Oracle's assertion of their non-rights to the Hudson continuous integration server has been resolved. In a detailed article at hudson-labs Andrew Bayer describes the decision to rename 'Hudson' to 'Jenkins'. Kohsuke and the community were remarkably patient in their attempts to resolve this with Oracle. It's sad that it's come to this, but given Oracle's character, it was inevitable. Oracle is certainly consistent in maintaining their reputation.
January 12, 2011
