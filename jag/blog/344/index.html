Spending a little time in Iceland

I'm spending the week in Iceland, wandering the country with my sister, assorted cousins, and some folks from Skyrr. The countryside is spectacular. It is totally unlike anything I've ever seen anywhere else. Totally volcanic, the rules of geology here are just different. Weird structures. Moonscapes. Phenomenal waterfalls. Unexpected lushness. Lovely!
