Too much!

Yesterday was mad. Today has been mad. All in a good way. Getting fresh stuff out on my blog has just been too hard. So many conversations with so many people. So much excitement. I can't walk 10 steps without someone cornering me to tell me about some cool thing they're working on. Exhausting and wonderful. So much energy.

My last "official" event yesterday was Canadian Night, where a bunch of Canadians got together to talk. Garry Rasko has been organizing these for several years. The high point for me was that he imported a sixpack of Big Rock Beer just for me, and by then, I needed it. After Canadian Night I headed off with my brother-in-law to burn the beer out of my system with some tasty Hunan food.

Needless to say, I haven't had anywhere near enough time to do prep for my keynote tomorrow. It'll be very improv, which will drive the stage crew mad. :-)
