Netbeans in Beijing

We just finished a special one-day event in Beijing focused on Netbeans, part of the world tour. We had a series of in-depth talks on a broad range of issues in Netbeans. The fellow who was supposed to be the moderator and first speaker had flight troubles (thunderstorms at Narita) that delayed him by a day so I ended up doing double duty: his parts and mine.
