NetBeans5 & Creator2

It's been an amazing week for the tools engineering teams. Creator 2 FCSed last week, and NetBeans 5 went out the door this morning. In my role as kibbitzer and tinkerer, I've had a lot of fun playing with both of them. I'm amazed at how easy and fast it is to slap together web apps with Creator. And there are so many things to love in NB5 that I hardly know where to begin (although the build-your-own-extension wizards are real high on my list). This afternoon it's time for pizza and beer... Congratulations to everyone on all the teams, they've both turned out beautifully.
February 2, 2006
