Kuala Lumpur

I spent last week in Kuala Lumpur where we did a Tech Days event in the convention center next to the Petronas Towers. KL is quite a city, and Malaysia is quite a country. A great turnout (definatly above plan!). I met a lot of great developers working on interesting projects. I visited some government ministries: they have a strong open-source commitment and they're applying it all over the place.

Great food too...
