Fun in Bangkok

I'm spending the week in Bangkok talking to developers, customers and the press. I did a keynote yesterday morning a the TechDays conference. The event was pretty seriously over-subscribed: the main keynote room could only fit 1300, and it was packed, with a pile more people in an overflow roon. Had a lot of fun, including pulling out my trusty 3-person slingshot to send t-shirts into the audience.

Do your own t-shirt hurling! There are only a few weeks left to get your proposal in to the JavaOne t-shirt hurling contest. Lots of fun, prizes, free JavaOne passes and a chance to show off your inner geek.
