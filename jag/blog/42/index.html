The coolest talk at JavaOne

My vote for the coolest talk at JavaOne is the one titled Mars Exploration Rover Operations with the Science Activity Planner. A release of this software, known as Maestro, was made publically available shortly after the first rover landed. Besides talking about the software in detail, they'll also have piles of cool images and will talk about the mission itself.
June 24, 2004
