The Art of Video Games

The Smithsonian institution is planning an exhibition called The Art of Video Games. Sun's former Chief Gaming Officer, Chris Mellisinos, has been a major driver behind the exhibit. It looks like it's going to be wickedly cool [at least, to those of you who like video games :-) ] But there's a problem: the funding situation is a little tight (surprise!!). So they're looking for donations. Please, if you can, give a few dollars to show that gaming is for real. And if you know anyone at a gaming company, prod them into making a serious donation by contacting Chris.
September 4, 2011
