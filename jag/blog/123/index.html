The real Matisse

A good place to get a bit of peace and quiet, if you've got a little spare time (I know, I know: the JavaOne schedule is way to packed) is the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art. It has half a dozen paintings by Matisse, as well a others by Magritte, Mondrian, Georgia O'Keefe, Jasper Johns, Frida Kahlo, Diego Rivera and many others. There's an exhibit of early photography (1800s) going on now that is not to be missed.
June 26, 2005
