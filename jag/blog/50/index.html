At the Apple WWDC

One of the happy bits of syncronization is that this year JavaOne and the Apple WWDC happened at the same time. WWDC is in the West Hall of Moscone Center, JavaOne is everywhere else. I did a short talk at WWDC that was a quick run through of the state of the Java universe, what's happening in tools, and how well Java and the Mac fit together. I did a little demo of NetBeans and Creator, running on my Mac. We really do care about cross platform support!
June 29, 2004
