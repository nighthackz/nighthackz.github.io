JavaOne is getting closer...

I know, it's six months away (it's happening in May 2006, rather than the usual June), but some deadlines are creeping up. The call-for-papers closes November 30th. Do a really good job on your abstracts: there are always far more proposals than available time slots. And it's time to start thinking seriously about ideas for the T-Shirt hurling contest. Cash! Free passes to JavaOne! You just have to figure out how to get some t-shirts into the audience. Last year was a real lesson in engineering: 2 out of 3 entries broke, but in interesting ways: one "over tested" and burned out his motor; the other had their IR sensors prematurly triggered by the photographers cameras. Surely you (yes you, the person reading this) can do better!
November 18, 2005
