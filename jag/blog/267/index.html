Get it while it's Hot! 6u10 goes beta!

It's been a huge effort, but JDK 6u10 has finally hit beta. This is a major milestone for us. We've even debated renaming 6u10 to JDK 7 - but it has no API changes and is totally focused on deployment and desktop integration, including coping with the-Joy-that-is-Vista. Congratulations to everyone involved. I saw some demos of parts of it yesterday (mostly applet stuff), and it was real sweet. Take it out for a spin.
