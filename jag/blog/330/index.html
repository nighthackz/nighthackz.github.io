Speaking at PayPal

I spent the morning speaking at an internal conference of PayPal's engineering organization. They had invited me to give a talk on how to do innovation. I don't know if I said anything useful in between the cynical rants :-) but I had a good time. They've got a great crew of engineers putting together a new generation of APIs for doing commerce. I had used their old APIs in previous projects, but when the current round of work on store.java.com got started in Sun we started using the new "adaptive" APIs. Lovely!
