Cruising through Toronto

I'm spending Monday and Tuesday in Toronto as part of the NetBeans world tour and TechDays events. We've been giving technical talks to piles of developers. Toronto is a pretty active place with a lot of talent doing interesting things.

We spent today going through all the new stuff in NetBeans 5, including my current favorite feature: the rapid plugin development tools. It's straightforward to build a (simple) plugin in 30 seconds, and deploy&test it. Far easier than writing emacs lisp extensions :-)
